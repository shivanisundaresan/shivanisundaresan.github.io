Hello! I'm Shivani Sundaresan.

I grew up in Chantilly, VA but spent my elementary and middle school years in Bangalore. I joined The University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 2021 where I found a passion for coding through my coursework and extracurricular involvements. I am currently double majoring in computer science and cognitive science, as it allows me to combine my passion for coding, math, psychology and philosophy.

On campus, I am the Vice President of Girls in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, a student organization with over 200 active members focused on empowering women and non-binary people to pursue careers in tech. My role involves organizing professional development events with our sponsors to help our members succeed in their future careers. I am also a data visualization project manager on BLUElab Metro, which is an engineering project team focused on socially engaged design solutions in the Ann Arbor/Detroit area. I'm a member of MProduct (Michigan's premier product management club) and a research group called MAISI (Michigan AI Safety Initiative).

Outside of college, I am involved with Youth for Privacy, an organization that works with the United Nations Open-ended Working Group on Information and Communications Technologies to advocate for data privacy rights.
